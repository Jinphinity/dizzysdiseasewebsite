# VISUAL & AESTHETIC STANDARDS

## Core Theme: Survival Horror & Biopunk
The overarching aesthetic for this project is a hybrid of **Survival Horror** and **Biopunk**. It must feel oppressive, organic, decaying, and grounded in a grim, low-tech reality. 

**Reference:** The browser game *Dead Frontier* serves as a primary structural and atmospheric reference.

### 1. Color Palette
Generations must strictly adhere to this constrained palette to maintain the oppressive atmosphere:
- **Primary Accent:** Blood Red (`#8A0303` to `#4A0000`). Used for critical interactions, alerts, and vital fluid themes.
- **Secondary Tone:** Dark, Dusky Phthalo Green (`#1A2F22` to `#0A150F`). Used for shadows, ambient lighting (e.g., retro monitors, bioluminescence), and decay.
- **Backgrounds/Base:** Deep Charcoal and almost-black greys (`#121212`, `#1C1C1C`). Avoid pure black (`#000000`) to retain texture visibility.
- **Text/Highlights:** Muted, desaturated off-whites or bone hues (`#D0CFC5`). Avoid pure white.

### 2. UI & Web Design Sensibilities
When generating HTML/CSS or designing user interfaces:
- **No Clean Lines:** Avoid modern, flat, "tech" aesthetics (unless specifically requested for an in-universe retro OS). Interfaces should feel tactile and in-world (diegetic).
- **Tattered Pages:** Use textures of torn, stained, or aged paper for text containers, menus, and notes. Edges should be rough.
- **Mechanical Typewriter:** Typography should heavily favor mechanical, monospace, or typewriter-style serif fonts (e.g., Courier, Special Elite, VT323). Text rendering should feel analog, imperfect, or stamped.
- **Grime and Decay:** Add subtle CSS noise, vignette, or texture overlays to prevent the screen from looking "clean."

### 3. Image & Video Generation
When prompting image or video models for this project:
- **Lighting:** Low-key, high-contrast, chiaroscuro lighting. Light sources should feel harsh and isolated.
- **Texture:** Focus on wet, organic, decaying textures mixed with rusted, brutalist, or industrial medical equipment.
- **Subjects:** Mutants, zombies, bio-mechanical anomalies, abandoned laboratories, blood splatters, and decaying urban environments.
- **Perspective:** Grounded camera angles. Avoid clean, clinical, top-down isometric views unless rendering a specific map/blueprint.

**Mandate:** Before generating any visual asset (code or imagery), ensure it complies with this baseline. If the prompt does not specify a style, default to these Survival Horror constraints.
