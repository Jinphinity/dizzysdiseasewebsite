# Website Assignment: Creating a 5-Page Website (25%)

**Assignment Overview:**

In this assignment, you will create a five-page website for a company of your choice. This website will include a blog section where you will write posts related to the industry / product and the company you have chosen. The goal of this assignment is to apply your Owned Media skills to design a user-friendly, informative, and engaging website.

# Requirements:

1.      **Home Page**: This page should provide an overview of the hospitality company, including its mission, vision, and key offerings. It should be visually appealing and user-friendly.

2.      **About Us Page**: This page should include detailed information about the company’s history, values, and team members. It should provide a comprehensive understanding of the company’s background and ethos.

3.      **Services Page**: This page should outline the services offered by the company. Each service should have a brief description and any relevant pricing information.

4.      **Contact Us Page**: This page should provide contact information, including phone number, email, and physical address. It should also include a contact form for visitors to send inquiries directly through the website.

5.      **Blog Page**: This page should feature at least two blog posts related to the hospitality industry and the chosen company. Each blog post should be well-researched, relevant, and engaging.

# Technical Requirements:
·         Include at least one image or graphic on each page.
·         Ensure that all links are functional and lead to the correct pages.
